HISTORY OF TETRIS GAME

Tetris is the addictive puzzle game that started embracing our universal desire to create order out of chaos. The Tetris game was created by Alexey Pajitnov in 1984 from it early programming experience and his love of puzzles. Pajitnov titled the game Tetris,a word created from a combination of "tetra" (meaning "four") and his favorite sport, "tennis". In the decades to follow, Tetris became one of the most successful and recognizable video games, appearing on nearly every gaming platform available.
